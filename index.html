Development Potential

Utilization Score

The potential for development score is generated by weighting and summing up factors such as zoning, connectivity, flood risk, utility service, minimum size, average slope of parcel, environmental remediation, and current use/ownership. Current use was established using Microsoft Canadian building footprints, satellite imagery, local knowledge and given a score from 1 to 4.

  • 1 - In use long term by known entity
  • 2 - In use, term unknown
  • 3 - In use, term unknown, potential for redevelopment
  • 4 - Vacant lot, appears not to be in use

The resulting cumulative development score ranges from 8 to 19 with the higher scores signifying a higher potential for development or redevelopment.


Data accurate as of April 2022.
