Utilization Score

The utilization score is generated by weighting and summing up factors such as zoning, connectivity, flood risk, utility service, minimum size, average slope of parcel,and current use/ownership. Current use was established using Microsoft Canadian building footprints, Satelite Imagery, and local knowledge and given a score from 1 to 4.

  • 1 - In use long term by known entity
  • 2 - In use, term unknown
  • 3 - In use, term unknown, potential for redevelopment
  • 4 - Vacant lot, appears not to be in use

Data accurate as of April 2022.
